# HR Analytics & Employee Attrition Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The HR Analytics & Employee Attrition Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition patterns and workforce behavior. This dashboard helps organizations identify key factors influencing employee turnover and supports data-driven HR decision-making.

The project focuses on analyzing attrition trends based on departments, age groups, overtime, salary, job satisfaction, and employee risk categories.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Employee attrition is one of the major challenges faced by organizations. High attrition impacts productivity, increases hiring costs, and affects overall business performance.

This dashboard was created to:

* Identify departments with high attrition
* Analyze factors contributing to employee turnover
* Understand employee demographics and behavior
* Provide actionable HR insights through data visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Employee Attrition KPI Tracking
* Department-wise Attrition Analysis
* Attrition by Age Group
* Salary vs Attrition Analysis
* Overtime Impact on Attrition
* Attrition Risk Segmentation
* Interactive Slicers & Filters
* Key Influencers Analysis
* Decomposition Tree Analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Research & Development department recorded the highest employee attrition.
* Employees working overtime showed significantly higher attrition probability.
* Younger employees (21–30 age group) were more likely to leave the organization.
* Lower salary and poor work-life balance strongly influenced attrition trends.
* Job satisfaction played a major role in employee retention.
